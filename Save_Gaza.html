Current Situation in Gaza

The ongoing crisis in Gaza has led to a severe humanitarian emergency. Thousands of civilians are facing critical shortages of basic necessities, including food, clean water, and medical supplies. Healthcare facilities are overwhelmed, and infrastructure damage has left many without shelter.

Scale of the Crisis

The humanitarian situation has reached critical levels. Essential services are severely disrupted, affecting millions of civilians. Medical facilities are operating beyond capacity, and access to basic necessities has become increasingly challenging for the local population.
